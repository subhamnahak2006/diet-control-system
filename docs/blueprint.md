# **App Name**: NutriMind AI

## Core Features:

- Personalized Health Profile Input: A user-friendly form to capture essential health details including age, gender, height, weight, activity level, fitness goals, existing diseases, food preferences, allergies, and sleep patterns.
- Dynamic Metric Calculation: Automatically computes the user's Body Mass Index (BMI) and estimates their daily calorie requirements based on the provided health profile.
- AI-Powered Diet Plan Tool: Generates a detailed, day-long meal plan (breakfast, mid-morning snack, lunch, evening snack, dinner) customized to the user's dietary preferences, allergies, medical conditions, and fitness objectives.
- AI Lifestyle & Hydration Advice Tool: Provides tailored recommendations for daily hydration intake and offers practical tips for improving sleep quality, assessing the user's current sleep duration and consistency.
- Comprehensive Health Report Display: Presents a structured, easy-to-read summary of the user's health status, including BMI results, daily calorie goals, the personalized diet plan, a list of foods to avoid, and actionable lifestyle/health tips.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary brand color: A deep, invigorating forest green (#33734C), evoking nature, health, and vitality, used for key UI elements and calls to action.
- Background color: A very subtle, almost off-white green (#ECF7F0), providing a clean, fresh, and calming canvas that complements the primary color.
- Accent color: A vibrant lime green (#A1E029), chosen for its energetic contrast to highlight important information, interactive elements, and positive affirmations.
- Headline and body font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif, offering a modern, clear, and objective aesthetic that ensures readability for health information and recommendations.
- Utilize clean, modern, and line-based icons for all navigational elements and informational sections (e.g., apple for food, water drop for hydration, moon for sleep).
- Employ a responsive, grid-based layout that prioritizes clear information hierarchy and readability, using ample whitespace to reduce cognitive load on the user.
- Incorporate subtle, smooth transitions and hover effects to guide user interaction and enhance engagement without being distracting, especially when displaying generated reports.
